“Why should we study Mathematics”

Mathematics doesn't seem very useful in real life and you just give up on it.
But if I tell you that Mathematics is very useful in real life.

“Math in our daily life”

Math is use in various of jobs or career, everyone in the world use mathematics.
Everything in the world is made of mathematics.
Mathematics can make beautiful things like “The Golden Ratio”
I will show you 5 examples that math is literally every where in our daily life.

Shopping

You would need to calculate the amount money that you will pay.
Or calculating how much money in account after paid.
Not a very serious task but it needs Math to operate

Cooking

You need to measure the ingredients that in the recipe.
Or your food will be thrown away.
Because cooking isn’t a simple task, You can’t just throw a ton of sugar in it.

Home decoration

Sometimes you just want your house to looking clean. So you decide to decorate the house.
Home decoration also need math, to properly place the items, calculating the area or length. Like placing sofa, Placing television, etc.
Or your sofa won’t fit in your house well and you just wasting your own money.

Time management

Time management is nothing without time calculation.
You need to calculate the time or date to manage your time.
Or you will forget to do your homework.

Driving

Even driving, you need math.
Calculate how long you will reach your friend’s house or When to increase or decrease speed.
Or you will late to your friend’s party
